Card AI can be reviewed without a pre-provisioned demo account.

1. On first launch, choose "Skip and start now" to enter as a guest. Guest sessions are real server-backed accounts and can use Home, Search, Scan, Collection, Profile, and account deletion.
2. A new guest may have an empty Portfolio. Use Search to inspect the production card catalog, or use the center Scan tab to capture/import a card and review recognition candidates. A card is not added until the reviewer confirms it.
3. Scan detects and crops the card area on device. The cropped card image is uploaded to private Cloudflare storage for scan records, support, and recognition-quality review, and is automatically deleted within 30 days. The external recognition provider receives perceptual hashes, not the image.
4. Sign in is optional. Native Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In are available from "Sign In / Sign Up". Email registration requires the verification code delivered to the entered address.
5. Guest deletion: Profile > Delete Account. Signed-in account deletion: Profile > account row > Delete Account. Both paths require confirmation and delete the current account data, including stored scan images.
6. Card prices and portfolio values are informational collection-management data, not financial or appraisal advice.
7. Version 1.0 contains no subscriptions, in-app purchases, paid digital content, or Restore Purchases flow.

Camera and photo-library access are requested only when the reviewer uses the related Scan action. Terms, Privacy Policy, Customer Support, Score, and Share are available from Profile.
