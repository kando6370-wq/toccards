Card AI helps trading card collectors identify cards, organize their collection, and follow market information.

SCAN WITH CONTROL
Capture a card or choose photos from your library. Card images are cropped and hashed on your device. The cropped card area is uploaded to Card AI's private service for scan records and recognition-quality review, retained without a fixed expiration period, and never sent to the external recognition provider. Review suggested matches before anything is added to your portfolio.

ORGANIZE YOUR COLLECTION
Track quantities, conditions, grading details, purchase values, and notes. Use folders to keep different parts of your portfolio organized, and save cards to your Wishlist for later.

FOLLOW VALUE
View current market information, price history, and portfolio changes in your selected currency. Card and price information may be incomplete or delayed and is provided for collection management, not financial advice.

USE IT YOUR WAY
Start as a guest or sign in to keep an account. Manage preferences, contact support, review the Terms and Privacy Policy, and delete your account from within the app.

Card AI currently focuses on trading card collection workflows, including scanning, search, card details, Portfolio, Wishlist, and market context.
